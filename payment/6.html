Features
 
1. Wallet-to-Wallet Transfer

Flash USDT can be transferred directly from wallet to wallet, creating a secure and fast transaction process.
Transfer Chain Limit: A maximum of 60 transfers in a chain. For example, if you send Flash USDT to someone, and they send it further, this can continue for up to 60 transfers before it reaches its expiration.

2. Expiry Period

Flash USDT has an expiry period of 140 to 160 days from the purchase date. After this period, it will automatically disappear from the wallet.

3. Reselling Flash USDT

If your Flash USDT has been in your wallet for 30 days or more, you can resell it back to us at the same rate you purchased it. The buying/selling process is completed securely via email, as detailed in the About section of the platform.

4. Equivalent to Real USDT

Flash USDT is equivalent to real USDT. It can be used on platforms like gambling sites just like the real thing. However, keep in mind that Flash USDT can be detected by systems that track USDT transactions.

5. Not Compatible with P2P Transactions

Flash USDT cannot be used in peer-to-peer (P2P) transactions. It is strictly limited to internal transfers.

6. No Withdrawal Capability

Flash USDT cannot be withdrawn. It is designed for use within the platform for buying and reselling or for use in specific applications like gift card purchases.

7. Flash USDT for Binance, TRC20, and Ethereum Networks

Flash USDT is available on Binance, TRC20, and Ethereum (ETH). The platform allows users to buy and transfer Flash USDT in these formats securely, offering flexibility for those who prefer different blockchains.

8. Gift Card Purchases

If you're using Binance Flash USDT, you have the option to purchase gift cards as well! Simply follow the steps within the platform to redeem your Flash USDT for a gift card.
